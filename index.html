SCHOOL MISSION

To provide a quality holistic education to the young people in preparation for life in this world and the world to come.

The school operates a system to ensure that its youth may receive a balanced physical, mental, moral, social and vocational education in harmony with denominational standards and ideals with God as the source of all moral value and truth. His revealed mind and will are the criteria for right and wrong. The stated interest of the school is in the optimum development of the whole child for both this life and the life hereafter.

HISTORICAL BACKGROUND

Segero Adventist School - Eldoret is located in Uasin Gishu County, Kapseret Sub County, approximately 15 km from Eldoret town along the Eldoret â€“ Kisumu road. The school is approximately 1 km from Kapseret Centre. It was established in the year 2012 as a school owned and run by the Greater Rift Valley Conference of the Seventh-Day Adventist Church. It started as a mixed boarding school with 51 students and 7 teachers. The school is duly registered by the Ministry of Education, Science and Technology as a triple-streamed school, Registration Number 27P30000140. The school is fully owned by the Seventh-Day Adventist Church which serves as the manager and the sponsor. The management is delegated to the Board of Management appointed by the school constituency. The current Chairperson of the BOM is Dr. Joshua Manduku, who is a university lecturer.
The objective of the school is to provide quality holistic education in spiritual, academic, physical and social spheres for relevance in a competitive environment. The main source of finance is school fees. However, the school supplements this through other income earned from the farm and bakery. The school plays a major role in the integration of faith and learning and proclamation of the gospel within the school community and its neighbours. The school currently has 278 students, 17 teachers and 11 non-teaching staff. The school boasts of impressive KCSE results over the years and it was the top school in national exams in the year 2019 in Kapseret Sub County. The current principal is Mr. Job Onyango.
